# Realists Training Technologies

Realists Training Technologies develops software and simulation systems for surgical training, medical education, and MedTech product demonstrations.

Our work focuses on building realistic and practical tools that support training, product understanding, and technical education in the field of spine procedures.

Based in Leipzig, Germany. 🇩🇪
